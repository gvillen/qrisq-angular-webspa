Location-Specific Hurricane and Wind Storm Surge Data

Q-risq Analytics forecasts Hurricane and Wind & Storm Surge risk at specific locations - days before the the storm makes landfall - to help you prepare in advance.

Q-risq Analyticps patented process equips you with site specific pre- and post-storm analysis, so you know what could happen, how to assess damage, and how to recover faster.

Prior to landfall

Days before landfall, the Q-risq Analytics team is working at full capacity, cont inuously monitoring the storm’s track and intensity to provide location specific wind and surge forecast data.

Post-Event Analysis

Once the storm has made landfall, Q-risq Analytics focuses on creating a report of the storm’s impact. Maximum sustained winds, storm surge, and accumulated rainfall are displayed for each address.
